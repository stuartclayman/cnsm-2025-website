Venue and Accommodation

CNSM 2025 will be held in the vibrant city of Bologna, Italy, at DAMSLab, a dynamic cultural and educational space managed by the University of Bologna.

Venue Address

DAMSLab - University of Bologna
Piazzetta P. P. Pasolini 5b
Bologna, Italy
Map

DAMSLab is situated in Bologna’s historic city center, within walking distance of many cultural landmarks, restaurants, and hotels. Known for its rich academic heritage and lively atmosphere, Bologna offers an ideal setting for international collaboration and networking.

The main conference and mini-conference sessions will take place in the DAMSLab Auditorium, a modern venue equipped for lectures and multimedia presentations.

Some of the Monday workshops will be hosted in the Centro Polifunzionale UNIone, located at via Azzo Gardino, 33 (5 minute distance - Map).

For detailed room assignments and session locations, please refer to the Technical Program.

Accommodation

Participants of CNSM 2025 are responsible for booking their own accommodation in Bologna. To assist with your planning, we are pleased to share a few suggestions with special discounted rates. However, you are free to choose any accommodation that best suits your preferences and budget.

Important: Bologna is a popular destination for conferences, exhibitions, and cultural events. We strongly recommend booking your accommodation as early as possible, as availability can become limited well in advance.

Additional Tips

Bologna offers a wide range of accommodation options, from hotels and boutique guesthouses to apartments and hostels. Most central accommodations are within walking distance or a short bus ride from the conference venue.

We encourage you to explore accommodation platforms such as Booking.com, Airbnb, or the Bologna Tourist Office website for additional options.
